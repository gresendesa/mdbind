## Anticipated blockers and mitigations

- Blocker: Suppressing external refs in `--file` mode could hide real broken
  refs.
  Mitigation: keep same-file broken refs deterministic and preserve
  repository-wide validation through `--root`.
- Blocker: JSON consumers may rely on `broken_ref` from file mode.
  Mitigation: prefer additive or clearly documented behavior and cover JSON
  output in tests.
- Blocker: The test inversion criterion could be ambiguous if expressed only
  as prose.
  Mitigation: record the old failing expectation in the sprint log and keep the
  committed test suite focused on the corrected behavior.
